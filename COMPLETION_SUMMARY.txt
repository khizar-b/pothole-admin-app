## 🔑 Key Features Explained

### Impact Score Formula
```
impact_score = (area_normalized × 0.6) + (confidence_score × 0.4)
```

**Example:**
- Pothole size: 45×50 pixels → 0.73% of image (area_normalized)
- Detection confidence: 0.95 → 95% (confidence_normalized)
- Impact Score: (0.73 × 0.6) + (95 × 0.4) = 38.4
- Level: MEDIUM impact for both bikes and cars ⚠️

### CSV Upload Changes
**Before:**
```
image_name, timestamp, bbox_x, bbox_y, bbox_width, bbox_height, 
confidence_score, damage_type, latitude, longitude
```

**After:**
```
image_name, timestamp, bbox_x, bbox_y, bbox_width, bbox_height, 
confidence_score, latitude, longitude
(Impact scores calculated automatically!)
```

### Records Table Updates
**Old Columns:**
- ID, Image, Timestamp, Damage Type, Confidence, GPS, Actions

**New Columns:**
- ID, Image, Timestamp, Confidence, Impact Score, Bike Impact, Car Impact, GPS, Actions
